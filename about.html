Alex Naghavi is an award-winning, tech-forward, and culture-driven Creative Director with a rich background in digital product, branding, and venturing. Based in Los Angeles, Alex’s career spans over sixteen years, grounded in purpose, beauty, and simplicity, while consistently striving to promote meaningful change and positive disruption.

Renowned for her multidisciplinary expertise, Alex seamlessly integrates design, technology, culture, business, and leadership. Her outstanding contributions to the field earned her a place on Creative Boom's 2015 list of the ‘20 Influential Designers You Need To Know’. She has played a pivotal role in shaping the digital products of numerous early-stage startups, including We Are Hunted (acquired by Twitter), Clipchamp (acquired by Microsoft), and Vest (acquired by VentureCrowd). She also led the redesign of the global social platform Myspace in 2012 and the RCA Records brand refresh in 2021.

Over the past decade, Alex has been at the helm of global design and venture studio, Josephmark, where she has spearheaded projects across fintech, art-tech, sextech, music, entertainment, mental health, Space, education, AI, AR and conversational design. Under her leadership, Josephmark received the prestigious Boutique Agency of the Year recognition from the Art Directors Club Awards in 2023. Her diverse range of clients includes Google, Redbull, AMC, Sony, Spotify, Hasbro, XPRIZE, Twitter, Bruce Springsteen, Elvis, and Portia De Rossi.
